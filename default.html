Gameplay

When encountering a chest during your travels, it contains a valuable treasure: a powerful sigil that can significantly impact future battles. Be sure to collect the loot to enhance your capabilities.

Restoring balance to the forest and healing the corrupted trees earns you the favor of Gaia. She then summons guardians of the forest to assist you. These guardians possess immense strength, helping to turn the tide of battles.

When enemies are defeated, their essence temporally increased energy, mana, or power. By doing so, it becomes possible to boost one's capabilities significantly, using the collected energy to enhance Sigil powers and duration.

These precious gems significantly enhance your Sigils, enabling you to execute more powerful magic. Seeking out these gems during quests can lead to increased energy, mana, and overall power levels.

Dragon eggs can be used to create powerful guardian Sigils, ready to help you turn the tide of any battle. Each Sigil grants you access to a guardian of incredible power, poised to be unleashed in your time of need.



Dragon Sigils

Wyrms serve as ranged sentries, providing an effective means to defeat enemies from afar. They offer a reliable source of base damage.

Guardians offer dual functionality in battle, acting as both protectors to absorb enemy attacks and as powerful sources of damage. 

Harness the power of your primary dragon guardian to unleash devastating attacks and strategically target enemies. Gain an enhanced perspective of the battlefield for optimal control and impact.

As a dragon use your powerful claws to attack multiple enemies simultaneously and boosts your speeds you to dodge attacks with ease to make strategic strikes against your opponents.

Soar through the skies as a flying dragon, using your fiery breath to defeat enemies and explore areas otherwise unreachable.

Sigil spells

Use defensive Sigils to control the battlefield and avoid damage from your enemies.

Enhance your guardian damage with location-based offensive Sigils, a powerful supplement for strategic gameplay.

Unleash your power with action Sigils and conquer your foes. Channel dragon magic through your weapon of choice, gun, bow, or dynamic movements to dominate the battlefield.

Master the Art of Druidic Power and Sigils

Take on the role of the Dragon Druid and explore a richly detailed realm filled with challenging encounters and diverse environments. Using ancient sigils, magic and dragon abilities, you will progress through a carefully designed fantasy adventure that rewards strategy and exploration.

Set out on a quest to collect rare dragon eggs, each connected to a powerful guardian that enhances your abilities. Your journey leads through treacherous mountain regions where crystal corrupted elementals block your path. Each encounter tests your skill and understanding of the game’s combat and movement systems.

All abilities are powered by ancient sigils. Spells and action based skills are activated by selecting a sigil that defines the ability you use. Whether casting magic, firing your bow, or triggering movement based actions, each ability begins with a sigil choice. Selecting the right sigil at the right time is essential for success.

Combat combines ranged attacks, magic, and close range encounters. Precise movement, dodging, and positioning are key to surviving increasingly difficult battles. When necessary, you can transform into a dragon form, gaining access to powerful physical attacks or fire based abilities to handle larger groups of enemies.

Test your mastery across four difficulty levels, each designed to challenge your understanding of the Dragon Druid’s mechanics. Progress requires adaptability, careful decision making, and consistent improvement.
