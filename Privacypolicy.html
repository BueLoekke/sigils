PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated 19.04.2023

This privacy policy governs your (the „User”) use of all our software, games and applications (the “Application(s)”) for all the devices that was created by Sigils ApS

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

1. Automatically Collected Information

The Application collects certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of device, operating system and broad regional data (country). All the collected data is aggregated and anonymous, thus cannot be used to identify the User.

2. Precise information about the User identity, their location and tracking data.

The Application does not collect any precise information about the User’s identity, User or device real time location nor any data that can be used to track the User’s activity.

3. Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No, only Sigils ApS has access to the collected data.

4. Advertising

Application does not use ads.

5. Children

The application may be inappropriate for children under the age of 13.

6. Security

Sigils ApS takes appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the Data. Data processing is carried out using computers and/or IT enabled tools, following organizational procedures and modes strictly related to the purposes indicated. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain. We limit access to this information to authorized employees only, in order to operate, develop or improve the Application. Please be aware that, although we provide highest security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.

7. Changes

Sigils ApS reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time by notifying its Users on this page and possibly within this Application and/or – as far as technically and legally feasible – sending a notice to Users via any contact information available to the Sigils ApS It is strongly recommended to check this page often, referring to the date of the last modification listed at the top. Should the changes affect processing activities performed on the basis of the User’s consent, Sigils ApS shall collect new consent from the User, where required.

8. Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to our processing of the collected information asset forth in this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at support@sigils.studio
